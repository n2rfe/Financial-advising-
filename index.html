Welcome to NoBroke

NoBroke is a simple to use tool to help anyone budget for their future.

Yearly Income Total yearly income from all sources before taxes.

Cost of Living Current total housing, utilities, and transportation costs.

Dependents Number of persons in your household dependent on your income.

Cost of Dependents Contribution to the cost of living of your dependents.

Debt Total outstanding debt.

Monthly Obligation Total Monthly repayment of debt.

Emergency Fund Amount set aside for unexpected events.

Retirement Savings Amount set aside for retirement.

Other Savings Any other savings set aside.
